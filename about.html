Our Mission

To build a strong community of
believers who support each other
in their faith journeys.

Who are we?

We are believers of all ages and backgrounds, united by our faith in Jesus Christ. Whether you're firm in your faith or seeking answers, you'll find a warm welcome here. Our group is a place of acceptance, where questions are encouraged, and everyone is supported on their spiritual journey

Our Vision

To spread the love and teachings of Jesus throughout Warsaw.

Our Values

  1. Biblical Truth. We are committed to the teaching and preaching of the Word of God as the foundation of our faith and daily living. Scripture guides our beliefs, shapes our character, and directs our decisions as individuals and as a church. Through sound, Spirit-led teaching, we seek to help believers grow in truth and maturity in Christ.
  2. Spirit-Filled Living.  As a Pentecostal church, we believe in the active work of the Holy Spirit in the life of every believer. We value spiritual empowerment, the gifts of the Spirit, and a vibrant relationship with God that transforms lives. Our worship and ministry create space for the Spirit to move, heal, guide, and renew hearts.
  3. Christ-Centered Worship. Worship is at the heart of who we are, bringing us together to honor God in spirit and in truth. We believe worship should be heartfelt, joyful, and rooted in reverence for Christ. Through praise, prayer, and the Word, we encounter God and are refreshed for daily life.
  4. Loving Community. We believe the church is a family where everyone is welcomed, valued, and supported. Relationships matter to us, and we strive to create an environment of love, care, and mutual encouragement. Through fellowship and service, we grow stronger together in faith and unity.
  5. Spiritual Growth and Discipleship. We are dedicated to helping believers grow spiritually and live out their faith with purpose. Discipleship equips individuals to follow Christ faithfully in every area of life. Through teaching, mentorship, and prayer, we encourage continual growth and transformation.
