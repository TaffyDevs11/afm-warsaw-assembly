Our Women's Ministry

At AFM Warsaw, the Women’s Ministry is a vibrant community where women are empowered to grow in faith, purpose, and spiritual maturity. It is a space where your gifts are celebrated, your voice is valued, and your journey with God is nurtured. Whether you are new to the faith or seeking to deepen your relationship with Christ, this ministry is designed to support and inspire you.

Through fellowship, prayer, and the study of God’s Word, women come together to encourage one another, share experiences, and build lasting relationships. Each gathering is an opportunity to be inspired, equipped, and strengthened to lead with love, wisdom, and courage in your family, workplace, and community. Here, women experience spiritual growth and practical support that empowers them to live out God’s purpose.

Join us and be part of a community where faith flourishes, connections are meaningful, and your impact in the Body of Christ is amplified. Discover encouragement, mentorship, and a sisterhood rooted in God’s love. Come, connect, grow, and step fully into the calling God has placed on your life.
